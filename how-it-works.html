Students

Students submit their application form on our website.

Assessment of Access to Education

Students sign up and we assess their access to education. Students are contacted to explain the next steps.

3

Tutor - Student Matching

Students are contacted by their allocated tutor for an initial, online meet.

4

Lessons Commence

Lessons commence with a free 1 hour trial lesson initially. If satisfied, a payment plan for 10 consecutive lessons is organised.

5

Continual Reflection

Ethical Education regularly checkin with all students to ensure their needs are addressed.

1

Tutors Apply

Tutors submit their application form on our website, including their final year 12 marks, previous experience and Working With Children Check details.

2

1st Round Interview

Tutors, who we deem to potentially embody our mission, are interviewed virtually by our Education Team.

3

Interview Review

“All interviews are reviewed by our Leadership Team to make a final assessment — a combination of the application and interview performance”.

4

Tutor Training

Successful tutors are welcomed to the Ethical Education team and are guided through some introductory training.

5

Lessons Commence

Tutors are allocated their student and commence lessons.
